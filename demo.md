The demo shows first the user interface, where some unassigned tasks are listed. Then the simulation in Webots is started and you immediatly see the bots move. Two bots go to the same aisle and one bot has to wait. The two other bots are entering the isles. Once the bots reach a certain point in the aisle, they return to their drop off point. They then continue with the next task. During the execution i showed the warehouse map a couple of times to show the location of bots are also visable (only if the bots are in some places).

Somewhere in the demo i added some tasks whilst the program was running to show that that was also working.
